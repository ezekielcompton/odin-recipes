Compton Family Spaghetti

How to make spaghetti

My family's recipe for spaghetti is quick, cheap, and best of all, delicious!

    Ingredients
  • Noodles
  • Crushed tomato
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Bell Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Olive oil
    Steps
  1. Mince garlic, onion, bell pepper and carrots
  2. Saute until soft and fragrant
  3. Add tomato sauce and bring to a simmer
  4. In another pot, bring water to a boil. Add a liberal amount of salt to water
  5. Salt tomato sauce to taste, add italian seasoning
  6. When water boils add noodles and cook for ten minutes
  7. When snoodles are fully cooked drain, cover in sauce, and enjoy!
