Muffins, Man!

How to make muffins!

Who doesnt love muffins?

    Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Choclate Chips
    Steps
  1. Pre heat oven to 350 degres
  2. Mix dry ingreidents
  3. Make a well and slowly add wet ingreidents
  4. Line a muffin pan with liners
  5. Spoon batter into muffin liners
  6. Place in oven for 15 min
  7. The muffins are done when a fork inserted comes out dry. Remove from oven, let cool, and enjoy!
