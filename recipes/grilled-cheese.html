How to make grilled cheese

Easy lunch sure to be a crowd pleaser. My daughter even eats it

    Ingredients
  • Sourdough bread (you can use wahtever but sourdoughs just...better)
  • Cheese
  • Mayonaiise (dont doubt)
    Steps
  1. Heat a cast iron skillet to medium heat
  2. Coat all sides of bread with mayo. Yes its messy. Yes its worth it
  3. Quickly toast each piece of bread
  4. Flip each piece of bread. Add cheese and stack
  5. Press top down with back of spatula
  6. Remove and eat the thing!
